Adds the Damnation feats, some implementations may be a bit wonky.

Requires TT-Core.

I've enforced a pick order for the feats to make things more managable, it goes: Soulless Gaze > Mask of Virtue > Fiendskin > Maleficium.

Soulless Gaze 1 Feat: +2 to intimidate checks
Soulless Gaze 2 Feats: Frightens on first check and cowers if frightened on next check. Hard to implement RAW. 1 Round of duration per 5 over enemy DC
Soulless Gaze 3 Feats: +2 to intimidate checks
Soulless Gaze 4 Feats: Not implemented, can't work out how to get demoralize as a swift

Mask of Virtue 1 Feat: Makes alignment undetectable, might homebrew the rest

Fiendskin 1 Feat: 5 acid resist
Fiendskin 2 Feats: 10 acid resist
Fiendskin 3 Feats: Immunity to cold
Fiendskin 4 Feats: Immunity to acid, change type to Outsider(not sure if working)

Maleficium 1 Feat: +1 DC to evil descriptor spells
Maleficium 2 Feats: not implemented
Maleficium 3 Feats: +1 DC to evil descriptor spells
Maleficium 4 Feats: +2 caster level to evil spells
